Saperavi

Saperavi is a red dry table wine made with Saperavi grapes grown in the Kakheti region of Georgia. The wine has a natural cherry color, with slight pomegranate tones in the tears around the glass, a very impressive sight. The wine has different aromas of sweet cherry, raspberry tones prevail, long-lasting on the pallet.
